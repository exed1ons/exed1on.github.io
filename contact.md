## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you accept music submissions from artists?**  
A: Currently focused on established releases, but open to exceptional new work. Include streaming links, not attachments.

**Q: Can I use your reviews for promotional purposes?**  
A: Yes, with proper attribution. Link back to source article.

**Q: Why no comments section?**  
A: Keeping site static for simplicity. Use contact methods for discussion.

**Q: Do you monetize the site?**  
A: No. This is passion project and educational exercise.

**Q: How did you discover [obscure band]?**  
A: Combination of music blogs (Pitchfork, Stereogum, Polish sites), YouTube rabbit holes, Bandcamp browsing, and friend recommendations.
